Lessons & Coaching

Tennispark Buitenveldert

The Tennis School Buitenveldert is excited to offer a dynamic program for enthusiastic tennis players of all ages, aimed at elevating your tennis experience. Winter lessons will commence in the week of October 1, 2024, and consist of a series of 20 lessons. Choose from private lessons at €2,360, group lessons with four players at €629, or group lessons with six players at €482. Join them at our convenient location at Van der Boechorststraat 38, 1081 BT Amsterdam, and take your tennis skills to the next level! For more details, visit their website.

Tennis Club Kattenlaan

Tennis Club Kattenlaan offers an exceptional year-round playing experience with its outdoor courts and a variety of coaching programs designed for all skill levels. Starting in early October, players can participate in an 18-week program that includes group lessons with four players for €445, group lessons with three players for €595, or private lessons at €75 each. The club also hosts social events and competitions, fostering a vibrant community for both recreational and competitive players. Join them at Kattenlaan 13, 1054 KA Amsterdam, and elevate your tennis game! For more details, visit their website.

Amsterdam Tennis Academy

Amsterdam Tennis Academy is a premier tennis facility dedicated to providing high-quality training for players of all ages and skill levels. With a strong emphasis on technical development, physical conditioning, and mental resilience, the academy creates a supportive and engaging environment for all participants. The upcoming session runs from October 1, 2024, to April 15, 2025, spanning 20 weeks, with a price of €300 per term. Join them at IJsbaanpad 43, 1076 CV Amsterdam, and elevate your tennis skills! For more details, visit their website.
