A City That Loves Tennis!

Amsterdam is famous for its beautiful canals and rich history, but it also has a strong tennis community. Here are some interesting facts about tennis in this amazing city

Tennis History

Tennis in Amsterdam: Tennis became popular in the Netherlands in the late 1800s, and Amsterdam was one of the first cities to embrace the sport. The city has hosted many important tennis tournaments over the years.

Great Places to Play

Courts by the Canals: Imagine playing tennis with beautiful canal views! Clubs like the Amsterdamse Lawn Tennis Club offer stunning sights while you play.
