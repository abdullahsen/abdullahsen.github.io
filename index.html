Your ultimate guide for tennis in Amsterdam!

Whether you’re a seasoned pro, a weekend warrior, or just looking to pick up a racket for the first time, our guide is here to connect you with everything tennis-related in Amsterdam. From local clubs and courts to coaching and preparation for a game, we’ve got you covered!

A City That Loves Tennis!

Amsterdam is famous for its beautiful canals and rich history, but it also has a strong tennis community. Here are some interesting facts about tennis in this amazing city.

Tennis History

Tennis in Amsterdam

Tennis became popular in the Netherlands in the late 1800s, and Amsterdam was one of the first cities to embrace the sport. The city has hosted many important tennis tournaments over the years.

Great Places to Play

Courts by the Canals

Imagine playing tennis with beautiful canal views! Many clubs offer stunning sights while you play.

Friendly Community

Everyone Can Play

Tennis is for everyone in Amsterdam! Many clubs have events and tournaments that welcome players of all ages and skill levels. It’s a great way to meet new people.
