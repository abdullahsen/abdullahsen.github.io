Find Your Partner

Apps and websites to find tennis partners in Amsterdam:

Meetup

Meetup is a platform that allows people to create and join groups based on shared interests. You can find tennis groups in Amsterdam where members organize matches and events. Click here to visit Meetup.

RacketPal

Racket Pal is a dedicated app for finding tennis partners nearby. You can create a profile, set your skill level, and connect with other players in your area. Click here to visit RacketPal.

Sportpartner

This website connects sports enthusiasts looking for partners in various sports, including tennis. You can search for tennis partners based on location and skill level. Click here to visit Sportpartner.

Playtomic

Playtomic is an app designed for booking courts and finding playing partners. You can search for tennis players in your area and join matches or tournaments. Click here to visit Playtomic.

Facebook Groups

Several local Facebook groups focus on tennis in Amsterdam. You can join these groups to find partners, join events, or participate in discussions. Search for "Tennis in Amsterdam" on Facebook.

How to use these platforms

  • Create a Profile: Sign up and create a profile that includes your skill level and availability.
  • Join Groups: For platforms like Meetup and Facebook, join relevant groups to connect with other tennis enthusiasts.
  • Post Your Availability: Let others know when you’re available to play and what level of play you’re looking for.

Feel free to explore these options to find the perfect tennis partner in Amsterdam!
