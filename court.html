Find Your Court

Tennisclub Amstelpark

Tennisclub Amstelpark, situated at Koenenkade 8, 1081 KH Amsterdam, is a premier destination for tennis enthusiasts. The club boasts a range of high-quality courts, including both indoor and outdoor options, catering to players of all skill levels.

For those looking to play, court rentals are available at a competitive rate of €39 per hour. This makes it easy for individuals or groups to enjoy a match in a beautiful setting, regardless of the weather. The outdoor clay courts are perfect for those who enjoy the classic feel of traditional tennis, while the indoor courts offer year-round playability, ensuring you can enjoy the game no matter the season.

If you're interested in reserving a court, you can find more details on their official website at amstelpark.nl. The site provides comprehensive information on court availability, booking procedures, and additional club amenities. Whether you're looking to play a casual game or engage in a more competitive match, Tennisclub Amstelpark is an excellent choice for tennis lovers in Amsterdam.

Park Startbaan

Park Startbaan, located at Startbaan 2, 1185 XR Amstelveen, is a fantastic venue for tennis enthusiasts looking to enjoy the sport year-round. The facility specializes in indoor tennis court rentals, providing players with a comfortable environment to practice and play without the constraints of weather.

With an affordable rental rate of just €28 per hour, Park Startbaan makes it easy for individuals, friends, and families to book a court and enjoy a game of tennis. The indoor courts are designed to offer optimal playing conditions, ensuring that you can focus on improving your skills or simply having fun on the court.

For those interested in reserving a court, more information is readily available on their website at reserveren.parkstartbaan.nl. The website offers details on court availability, booking procedures, and any special promotions or events that may be happening. Whether you're a seasoned player or just starting out, Park Startbaan is an excellent choice for all your indoor tennis needs in Amstelveen.

Tennisvereniging Osdorp

Tennisvereniging Osdorp, conveniently situated at Han Hollanderlaan 1, 1067 BB Amsterdam, is a vibrant tennis club offering a fantastic range of facilities for players of all levels. The club features both indoor and outdoor tennis court rentals, making it an ideal destination for enthusiasts looking to enjoy the sport in any season.

The rental rates are structured to accommodate a variety of schedules. From Monday to Friday, you can book a court during the day from 09:00 to 18:00 for just €24 per hour. In the evenings, from 18:00 to 22:00, the rate is €30 per hour, perfect for those looking to unwind after work. On weekends, courts are available on Saturday and Sunday from 09:00 to 17:00 at a rate of €27 per hour, allowing you to enjoy a game during your leisure time.

For those interested in making a reservation or seeking more details about the club's offerings, you can easily visit their website at meetandplay.nl. The site provides comprehensive information about court availability, booking procedures, and any special events or programs that may be running. Whether you’re a seasoned player or just starting out, Tennisvereniging Osdorp is an excellent choice for your tennis needs in Amsterdam.
